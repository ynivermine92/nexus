How to Upload Your Art as an NFT on NFT Nexus

Bring your artwork to life as a Non-Fungible Token on [Brand Name]. Our platform makes it easy for artists and collectors alike to upload and sell their unique digital creations.

Follow these simple steps to get started:

Create an account on NFT Nexus

Click on the "Upload Art" button from the main menu

Select the image file of your artwork and provide a detailed description, including the title, artist name, and any other relevant information.

Choose the type of NFT you want to create, such as a single edition or a limited series.

Set your asking price for the NFT and choose any additional options, such as a bidding process or a fixed price sale.
